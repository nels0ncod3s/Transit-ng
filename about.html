About Us

Welcome to Naija Transit Guide — a platform built to help passengers in Nigeria navigate unfamiliar routes easily. Whether you’re new in town or simply exploring, our goal is to give you reliable directions and transport tips from real people who know the road.

We believe in the power of community. With your help, we crowdsource verified routes so no one gets lost or overcharged again.

From Lagos to Kano, from bus stops to hidden shortcuts — we’re mapping Nigeria together.
